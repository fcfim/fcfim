## 🚀 About Me

I hold degrees in both **Civil Engineering** and **Business Administration**, combined with a career in **Software Development**.

While most developers lack the technical context of the construction site or the economic vision of a business, I operate at the intersection of all three. My goal is not to "balance" these roles, but to **maximize** every vertex of this triangle:

1.  **Engineering:** Ensuring technical accuracy, normative compliance (ABNT NBR), and safety.
2.  **Administration:** Focusing on business logic, product scalability, and economic viability.
3.  **Development:** Delivering clean architecture and robust code.

I build tools to eliminate the "gap" in the industry—solving project errors, automating manual calculations, and optimizing workflows that pure developers often overlook.
